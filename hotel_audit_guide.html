Hotel Audit & Fraud Suite

A comprehensive overview of MiAuditOps' multi-layered anomaly detection architecture, designed specifically to secure hospitality revenue streams and eliminate front-desk manipulation.

The Extractor's Purpose

Hospitality front desks frequently suffer from sophisticated revenue leakage—where cashiers manipulate Check-In/Check-Out times within the Property Management System (PMS) to pocket cash payments. "Ghost stays," "Short-time Overlaps," and "Shift-Clearances" are virtually undetectable via standard accounting. MiAuditOps bridges this gap by combining raw Data Extractions with Time-Series Anomaly Detection.

The Three Pillars of Protection

1. Strict Overtime Policy Enforcement

The system cross-references the Expected Checkout Time (default 12:00 PM) against the Actual Realized Checkout timestamp. If a guest overstays, the engine calculates the exact hour differential minus management grace periods.

  • Fractional Detection: Automatically flags 1 - 4 hours of delay as Minor Risk.
  • Half-Day Charges: Over 4 hours rigorously escalates to a 50% Daily Rate surcharge.
  • Full-Day Critical: Delays over 12 hours convert the room to a full night's payment expectation.

2. Hybrid Double-Sales Matcher

When a room is booked multiple times on the exact same calendar date, cashiers often hide short-stays. The engine groups same-day entries and evaluates the Time-Overlaps. But it doesn't stop there.

Intelligent Auto-Clearance Workflow

If the system detects a manipulated double-booking, but the cashier did properly enter a price into the PMS Base Revenue stream (`Rate > 0`), the Engine will flag it but instantly mark it as ✓ AUTO-CLEARED. This prevents the master ledger from double-charging the cashier for money they truthfully declared. Only "zero-rated" or unpriced shadow-bookings are left highlighted in Red as true uncaptured fraud.

3. Stealth Early Check-Out Tracking

The ultimate counter-measure against "Ghost Stays." If guests check in between 12:00 AM (Midnight) and 6:59 AM, the platform generates a Critical Risk Alert.

Night-Shift "Ghost Clearance" Alert
Captures cashiers checking guests out before morning shifts arrive. (Out before 9:00 AM on the same day).

The Two-Hit Reconciliation System

Our platform ensures no discrepancy slips through the cracks by employing a sequential "Two-Hit" accounting flow.

01

First Hit: System Discrepancy

This is the immediate baseline check. The system calculates the total Expected Room Revenue exported from the PMS and subtracts the Actual Banked Cash/Transfers declared by the cashier. This first hit catches raw missing funds.

02

Second Hit: Master Variance

This is the forensic layer. The system takes the First Hit anomaly and adds all Uncaptured Fraud Value—this includes Zero-Rated Double Sales, unpriced Stealth Check-ins, and critically, all fractional and full-day Overtime Penalties. The result is the absolute Net Variance owed by the shift.

Comprehensive Report Generation

All findings are crystallized into professional, immutable PDF reports optimized for Senior Management and Board Reviews.

  • Detailed Rectification Logs: Tracks every edit made by an auditor.
  • Auto-Cleared Formatting: Shadow bookings recognized by the system print directly with `[AUTO-CLEARED]` strike-through aesthetics.
  • Expense Requisitions: Summarizes all verified payouts to show net remittable cash.
